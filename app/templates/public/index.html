About

Sounravel is a website that allows the user to get information about an audiofile. The main purpose was to be able to process the files without having to download additional software.
In its current form it is able to provide the following images: MEL Spectrogram , Spectrogram for determining Quality of audiofile, "Soundcloud" type of Spectrogram and Tempograph. In addition it shows Spotify's estimation of its tempo and key.
The application also generates four downloadable files: Left channel, Right channel, Instrumental and Vocals
The web page was developed by Fleischman Krisztian in order to receive my Bachelor's Degree. I am currently completing the last year at Transilvania University of Brasov. I have studied Electrical Engineering and Computer Science during the period of 2017-2021
